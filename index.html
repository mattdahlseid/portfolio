About/ME

I'm a curious, versatile creative who possesses a passion for developing engaging user experiences, sharing powerful stories and exploring the outdoors.

During nearly a decade in the newspaper industry I wrote award-winning features, designed and edited sports sections, had hundreds of photos published in print and online, produced podcasts, and much more.

I've long been interested in interactive storytelling, which has led me to pursue front-end development and UX design. I was awarded a Grow with Google Developer scholarship in early 2018 and recently completed Udacity's six-month Front-End Web Developer Nanodegree program.

I'm currently seeking opportunities to produce engaging, original work with a team of talented creators.
